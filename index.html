About Me

Hello! I'm Ahmed Dillo, a fourth-year Computer Engineering student at İnönü University in Malatya, Turkey. In the fall of 2024, I had the opportunity to study abroad through the Erasmus+ programme at the Military University of Technology in Warsaw, Poland. I have a strong interest in Robotics and Embedded Systems. Although I'm still at the beginning of my journey, I actively work on projects involving microcontrollers like Arduino to strengthen my hardware and programming skills. Recently, I’ve also started learning Web Development to expand my technical skillset. I mostly code in Python and Java, and I aim to build solid knowledge in embedded programming, automation, and web technologies.
